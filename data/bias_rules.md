**Halo-Effect Die**
<!-- Sanguine - thick edges -->
**Psychological:** Triggers when this die is live, and any other of your live dice lands on a 6  
_Includes normal rolling, as well as manual changing._  
[1, 2, 3, 3, 4, 4]

**Somatic:** Triggers when your roll contains zero 6s


**Dunning-Kruger Die**
<!-- Sanguine - tri corner -->
**Psychological:** Triggers when you bank an insult of only 1s  
[2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 6]

**Somatic:** Whenever you fumble, gain 1 **fumble-shield token** and 1 **breakthrough token**  
_(Prevented fumbles do not activate 'when you fumble' triggers)_


**Egocentric Die**
<!-- Sanguine - companion -->
**Psychological:** Trigger every time you deal exactly 1 damage  
[1, 1, 2, 2, 6, 6]

**Somatic:** At the start of each round, triggers twice for every debate that has passed


**Normalcy Die**
<!-- Phlegmatic - Inny -->
**Psychological:** Triggers when banked as part of a one-pair  
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

**Somatic:** Trigger after rolling only odd faces


**Just-World Die**
<!-- Phlegmatic - tri dots -->
**Psychological:** Trigger when banking four or more dice in a single insult.  
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

**Somatic:** Trigger after rolling only even faces


**Mere-Exposure Die**
<!-- Phlegmatic - pipe -->
**Psychological:** Triggers any time you bank an insult that contains a 1  
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

**Somatic:** Every time you roll a '1', place a counter on this card.  
When it reaches 6, remove it, and trigger this 3 times.


**Victimhood Die**
<!-- Choleric - crenelation -->
**Psychological:** Whenever you take more than 3 damage, roll a d6. If the result is < the damage you took, gain a **breakthrough token**.  
[X, 1, 2, 4, 5, 6]

**Somatic:** Whenever you lose a debate, gain a **eureka token**.


**Illusory-Control Die**
<!-- Choleric - plug -->
**Psychological:** Triggers every time you receive a token.  
_(cannot be triggered by its imbued emotion)._  
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

**Somatic:** Triggers every time you decrement a token.  
_(but not 'clearing' tokens) (cannot be imbued with an emotion that decrements)._


**Bandwagon Die**
<!-- Choleric - bee -->
**Psychological:** Triggers any time an **echo dice** is summoned by yourself or opponents.  
_(cannot be triggered by its imbued emotion)._  
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

**Somatic:** Increase your max live die count by 1.  
_You can convert one of your somatic dice back to psychological, and roll it alongside your default 6. (Bandwagon Die has no somatic trigger)_


**Narrative-Fallacy Die**
<!-- Melancholic - outy -->
**Psychological:** Triggers when banking a straight  
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

**Somatic:** Triggers every roll, if every live-die has the normal faces [1, 2, 3, 5, 6]


**Groupshift Die**
<!-- Melancholic - ball -->
**Psychological:** If you bank 5 or more dice, and still lose the debate, gain a **eureka token**.  
[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]

**Somatic:** If you bank 3 or less dice, and win the debate, gain a **breakthrough token**.


**Scapegoat Die**
<!-- Melancholic - tri stone -->
**Psychological:** If this die is live during a fumble, roll a d6: triggers that many times.  
[2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 6]

**Somatic:** If you successfully commit 3 separate insults in a single debate, gain a **eureka token**.
